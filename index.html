Vulnerability Assessments

An in depth review of your organizations current state using automated tools to identify open ports, services, and any associated known Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures (CVE's) in a detailed report.

Penetration Testing

Take your Vulnerability Assessments to the next level - Nanobyte Security will not only identify your vulnerabilities, but demonstrate how threats exploit these vulnerabilities in real-world attacks. Will be provided a detailed report with gathered evidence, including proof of concept and documented exploitation of vulnerabilities.

Red Teaming

Need to test your security operations center, or find gaps in your defenses? Nanobyte Security will demonstrate how threats evade detection, and perform targeted and sophisticated attacks against your employees, systems and networks.
