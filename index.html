Our Services

Vulnerability Assessments

An in depth review of current state of your systems and networks using automated tools to identify open ports, services, and any associated known Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures in a detailed report.

Penetration Testing

Take your Vulnerability Assessments to the next level - Nanobyte Security will not only identify your vulnerabilities, but demonstrate how threats exploit these vulnerabilities in real-world attacks. Will be provided a detailed report with all evidence, including proof of concept and actual exploitation of vulnerabilities identified.

Red Teaming

Need to test your operations center, or find gaps in your defenses? Nanobyte Security will demonstrate how threats evade detection, and perform targeted and sophisticated attacks against your employees, systems and networks.
